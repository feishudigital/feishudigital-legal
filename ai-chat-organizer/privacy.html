Privacy Policy

AI Chat Organizer — Chrome Extension — Last updated: February 2026

Your conversations stay in your browser. AI Chat Organizer does not collect, transmit, or store any of your chat content on external servers. All data is stored locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local.

What data we store

All data is stored locally in your browser and never sent to our servers:

  • Folder structure: Names, colors, and hierarchy of folders you create
  • Tags: Tag names, colors, and which conversations they are assigned to
  • Conversation metadata: Conversation IDs and titles (as displayed in the platform sidebar) and their folder/tag assignments
  • Prompts: Prompt templates you create or save
  • Settings: Your preferences (theme, sort order, sidebar position)

We do not store or read the content of your conversations. The extension only reads conversation titles from the platform sidebar to display them in the organizer.

Export feature

When you use the Export feature, the extension reads message content from the currently open conversation page to generate a Markdown or JSON file. This data is:

  • Processed entirely in your browser
  • Downloaded directly to your device
  • Never sent to any server

Network requests

The extension makes network requests only to:

  • Lemon Squeezy API (api.lemonsqueezy.com) — to validate Pro license keys. Only the license key is sent, no personal data or conversation content.

We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, telemetry, or any third-party advertising services. There is no server-side backend.

Platforms accessed

The extension runs on the following websites to provide its functionality:

  • chat.openai.com / chatgpt.com (ChatGPT)
  • claude.ai (Claude)
  • gemini.google.com (Gemini)

On these sites, the extension reads the conversation sidebar to detect conversation titles and IDs. It does not modify, intercept, or transmit any data to or from these platforms.

Permissions explained

  • Host permissions (chatgpt.com, claude.ai, gemini.google.com): To inject the organizer sidebar and read conversation titles from the platform navigation
  • storage: To save your folders, tags, prompts, and settings locally
  • alarms: To periodically revalidate Pro license status (once per hour)

Data deletion

Uninstalling the extension removes all locally stored data immediately. You can also clear extension data via Chrome's settings (Extensions > AI Chat Organizer > Details > Clear data).

Third-party services

  • Lemon Squeezy (payment processing) — lemonsqueezy.com/privacy

Children's privacy

AI Chat Organizer is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, the updated version will be posted here. Material changes will be noted in the Chrome Web Store listing.
