Privacy Policy

BayScout — Chrome Extension & SaaS — Last updated: February 12, 2026

BayScout is a tool for eBay sellers. We collect the minimum data necessary to provide keyword research, price tracking, seller monitoring, and alert features. Your eBay login credentials are never accessed or stored.

2. What data we process

2a. Data stored locally in your browser

The following data is stored in chrome.storage.local on your device and never transmitted to third parties:

  • Authentication tokens: JWT access token and refresh token for your BayScout account session
  • Email address: Cached locally to display in the extension popup
  • Usage stats: Daily lookup count and reset time (cached for UI display)

2b. Data stored on our servers

When you create a BayScout account, the following data is stored in our database (hosted on Supabase, see Section 5):

  • Account profile: Email address, subscription plan (Free or Pro), daily usage counter
  • Tracked items: eBay item IDs, titles, prices, images, and listing URLs you choose to track
  • Price history: Historical price snapshots for your tracked items
  • Tracked sellers: eBay seller names, listing counts, average prices, and feedback scores for sellers you monitor
  • Seller snapshots: Historical activity data for tracked sellers
  • Alerts: Alert rules you create (type, target, conditions) and triggered alert events
  • Keyword data: Saved keyword lists and cached keyword research results
  • Analysis history: Results of listing and search analyses you perform
  • Subscription data: Lemon Squeezy customer ID and subscription ID (for Pro plan management)

We do not access your eBay account credentials. The extension reads publicly visible data from eBay search and listing pages.

3. Network requests and third-party data processing

The extension and backend make requests to the following services:

3a. BayScout API (our backend)

Hosted on Fly.io (Fly.io, Inc.) in Frankfurt, Germany (bayscout-api.fly.dev). All user data, authentication, keyword research, tracking, and alert processing happens here. Fly.io acts as our infrastructure provider (data processor). Data is stored in the EU (Frankfurt region).

3b. Supabase (database & authentication)

Supabase, Inc. provides our database (PostgreSQL) and user authentication service. Your email, password hash, and all application data listed in Section 2b are stored on Supabase infrastructure. Supabase acts as a data processor on our behalf. See: Supabase Privacy Policy.

3c. eBay APIs

eBay Inc. provides marketplace data through its public APIs. We use:

  • eBay Autocomplete API: Keyword suggestions (no personal data transmitted)
  • eBay Browse API: Item details, prices, seller info (no personal data transmitted)
  • eBay Shopping API: Seller feedback scores (no personal data transmitted)

Only search queries and item/seller identifiers are sent to eBay. No personal user data is transmitted. See: eBay Privacy Policy.

3d. Lemon Squeezy (payments)

Lemon Squeezy, LLC processes payments for Pro subscriptions as Merchant of Record. When you purchase a Pro plan, Lemon Squeezy collects your name, email, and payment information. Lemon Squeezy is an independent data controller for payment data. Our backend receives webhook notifications containing your subscription status and customer ID (no payment card details). See: Lemon Squeezy Privacy Policy.

3e. Resend (email notifications)

Resend, Inc. sends alert notification emails on our behalf. When an alert triggers, your email address and the alert details (item name, price change) are transmitted to Resend for email delivery. Resend acts as a data processor. See: Resend Privacy Policy.

3f. AI services (optional, not yet active)

When AI features are enabled in the future, listing data you submit for optimization may be sent to OpenAI (OpenAI, Inc.) or Anthropic (Anthropic, PBC) for processing. No personal data is included — only eBay listing text (titles, descriptions, keywords). These features are clearly labeled and only activated by your explicit action. This section will be updated when AI features go live.

3g. cron-job.org (scheduled tasks)

cron-job.org triggers our alert-checking endpoint every 4 hours. Only our server endpoint URL is involved — no user data is transmitted to or from cron-job.org.

We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, telemetry, cookies, or any third-party advertising services.

4. Content scripts on eBay pages

The extension runs content scripts on eBay websites to provide its features:

  • Search Overlay: Reads search result data (titles, prices, seller names) from the eBay page to display market statistics
  • Listing Analyzer: Reads listing details (title, price, photos, item specifics) to generate a health score
  • Search Expander: Enhances search functionality on eBay pages

This data is processed locally in your browser or sent to our backend for analysis. It is not shared with any other third party.

5. Data transfer to third countries

Your core application data is stored on Supabase and Fly.io infrastructure. While our Fly.io server is located in the EU (Frankfurt), Supabase infrastructure may involve data processing in the United States.

  • Supabase: Data may be transferred to the US. Legal basis: Standard Contractual Clauses (Art. 46(2)(c) GDPR).
  • Lemon Squeezy: Payment data is processed in the US. Legal basis: Art. 49(1)(b) GDPR — necessary for the performance of the subscription contract.
  • Resend: Email delivery infrastructure may be located in the US. Legal basis: Art. 49(1)(b) GDPR — necessary for providing the alert notification service you configured.
  • eBay APIs: Requests to eBay servers (US). No personal data is transmitted — only search queries and item identifiers. Legal basis: Art. 49(1)(b) GDPR.

6. Legal basis for processing (Art. 6 GDPR)

  • Account creation and authentication: Art. 6(1)(b) — necessary for the performance of the service contract.
  • Item/seller tracking and price history: Art. 6(1)(b) — core service functionality you requested.
  • Alert notifications (email): Art. 6(1)(b) — necessary to fulfill alert rules you created.
  • Keyword research and analysis: Art. 6(1)(b) — core service functionality.
  • Usage metering (daily limit): Art. 6(1)(b) — necessary to enforce the Free/Pro plan terms.
  • Payment processing via Lemon Squeezy: Art. 6(1)(b) — necessary for the Pro subscription transaction. Lemon Squeezy processes data as independent controller.
  • Local browser storage (tokens, cache): Art. 6(1)(b) — necessary for session management.

7. Data retention

  • Local browser data: Retained until you uninstall the extension or clear extension data.
  • Account and tracking data: Retained for the duration of your account. You can delete individual tracked items, sellers, alerts, and keyword lists at any time through the dashboard.
  • Price history and snapshots: Retained as long as the associated tracked item or seller exists.
  • Account deletion: Contact us at feishudigital@gmail.com to request full account deletion. All associated data will be removed within 30 days.
  • Payment data at Lemon Squeezy: Retained according to Lemon Squeezy's retention policy and applicable tax/accounting laws.

10. Your rights under GDPR

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Right of access (Art. 15) — You can request a copy of all personal data we process about you.
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16) — You can request correction of inaccurate data. You can also edit tracked items, sellers, alerts, and keyword lists directly in the dashboard.
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17) — You can request deletion of your account and all associated data. For payment data, contact Lemon Squeezy.
  • Right to restriction of processing (Art. 18) — You can request that we restrict processing of your data under certain conditions.
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20) — You can request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object (Art. 21) — You can object to certain processing activities. You can also disable alerts or uninstall the extension at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at feishudigital@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.
