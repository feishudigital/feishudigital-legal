Privacy Policy

Readability Scorer Chrome Extension — Last updated: February 2026

Your text never leaves your browser. Readability Scorer processes everything 100% client-side. We collect zero data.

What data we collect

None. Readability Scorer does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or user content. Period.

How the extension works

All readability analysis (Flesch-Kincaid, Gunning Fog, Coleman-Liau, SMOG, ARI, Dale-Chall) is performed entirely on your device using mathematical formulas. No text is sent to any server.

  • No analytics or tracking scripts
  • No cookies
  • No user accounts required for the free tier
  • No content is ever transmitted over the network

Network requests

The extension makes zero network requests during normal use. The only exception:

  • Pro license validation: If you purchase a Pro license, the extension sends only your license key to Lemon Squeezy's API (api.lemonsqueezy.com) to verify your purchase. No text content, browsing data, or personal information is included in this request.

Data storage

The extension stores minimal settings locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local:

  • Your preferences (auto-analyze on/off, target score)
  • Daily usage count (for free tier limit)
  • Pro license key (if purchased)

This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the extension.

Permissions explained

  • activeTab: To detect and analyze text in the currently active tab's text fields
  • storage: To save your settings and usage count locally
  • alarms: To reset the daily usage counter at midnight

Third-party services

The only third-party service used is Lemon Squeezy for Pro license payments. Their privacy policy is available at lemonsqueezy.com/privacy.

Children's privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change this policy, the updated version will be included in the extension and noted in the Chrome Web Store listing.
